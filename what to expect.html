What is life like on mars?

Currently, Mars is still a hostile world that does not have a breathable atmosphere. It also is constantly being bombarded with radiation from the sun, making mars uninhabitable on the surface. Human civilization will be held in underground bunkers that have natural protection from radiation making mars inhabitable.
